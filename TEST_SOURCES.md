## Notes & Corrections Made

1. **Big 6 vs Big 5**: ServSafe's current (7th edition) material refers to the "Big 6" pathogens, adding Nontyphoidal Salmonella to the original 5. Test updated to reflect this.

2. **Danger Zone discrepancy**: The USDA uses 40°F–140°F while ServSafe uses 41°F–135°F. The test uses the ServSafe standard since that's what's tested on the exam.

3. **Practice test scoring**: The real exam scores 80 questions (10 are unscored pilot), requiring 60/80 (75%). Our practice test scores all 90 questions, requiring 68/90 (75%) — same percentage threshold.
